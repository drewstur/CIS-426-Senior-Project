Student Club
Information Systems Student Association (ISSA)
ISSA is a student organization that was establised in 2008 to help members in their professional, educational, and social development. The organization encourages members to interact with business practitioners, faculty, and fellow students on information systems topics. All current PUC students are welcome to join.
Activities:
Internship and Career Path Workshops
Networking Events
IT/IS Conferences and Conventions
"Teaching Information Systems" Volunteer Opportunities
Certification Study Nights
Round Table Conferences
