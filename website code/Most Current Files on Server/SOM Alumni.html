Alumni
Purdue Alumni Association - Calumet serves as a liaison between its members, University alumni, and current Purdue Calumet students. It provides opportunities for networking and enhancing professional, personal and leadership development in efforts to increase loyalty to Purdue Calumet, foster pride in a Purdue degree and enhance students' Purdue Calumet experience.

Purdue Calumet's Gift To You
Membership is free for the first year after receiving your first degree from Purdue Calumet. That's our way of welcoming you into the Purdue alumni family.  So stay connected, renew your membership today!

Alumni are one of Purdue Calumet's greatest resources. Purdue Calumet alumni are located in all 50 U.S. States and in almost 20 foreign countries. With today's job market being increasingly competitive, mentors are in higher demand. As an alum, when you contribute your insight and energy to your alma mater, you not only affirm the quality of your education and your degree's impact on your life, you promote the reputation and success of Purdue Calumet and its students.
The PAAC Alumni Career Network is a partnership between the Purdue Alumni Association - Calumet and Purdue Calumet's Center for Career and Leadership Development.

Becoming a Member
Get two for the price of one! When you become a member of the Purdue Alumni Association - Calumet, you automatically gain membership into the Purdue Alumni Association, Inc. from Purdue University in West Lafayette, IN.
Send your check or money order made payable to the Purdue Alumni Association to the Office of Alumni Relations, Purdue University Calumet, 2200 169th Street, Hammond, IN 46323-2094
